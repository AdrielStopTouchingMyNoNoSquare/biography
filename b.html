Short Biography

Arthur Morgan is the primary protaganist in Rdr2. He is known as "Black Lung" because he has turberculosis and back in the Wild West it was an incurable disease. Arthur used to be married with 1 wife and a son but they were killed during a robbery. He is apart of the "Van der Linde Gang". Arthur is a man searching for purpose while constantly getting into criminal activities even though he's a good man at heart as people around him used to say. He died shortly after his gang leader Dutch Van der Linde killed him in a hill.
